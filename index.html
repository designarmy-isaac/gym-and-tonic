About Us

Welcome to Gym & Tonic, a unique blend of high-end athleisure apparel located in the heart of Park Potomac. The distinctively-designed store features fashionable, top-of-the-line clothing that's always stylish — whether you’re at the gym in the morning or out on the town at night.

Gym & Tonic is a labor of love of two retail veterans, Amy Block and Wendy Abelman. It's combining the best of Miami with the best of New York to offer something that’s truly new and fresh.
